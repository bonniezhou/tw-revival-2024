IT Bodoni

Mariya Lish

IT Bodoni is a revival of the famous Bodoni typeface, named after its creator, Giambattista Bodoni, an Italian typographer, printer, and type designer. Developed in the late 18th century, the Bodoni typeface is renowned for its elegance, modernity, and distinctive style, securing its place as a classic in typography.

One of the most striking features of Bodoni is its high contrast between thick and thin strokes, which gives the typeface a refined and sophisticated look. As a neoclassical typeface, Bodoni emerged during the Enlightenment period, an era that emphasised clarity, rationality, and simplicity. These qualities are beautifully reflected in Bodoni's design.

Mariya is a creative professional specialising in type design, with a focus on Latin and Cyrillic type design and development. Born in Minsk, Belarus, she has lived in the UK for over 18 years. Mariya is passionate about innovation and experimentation in the field of type design.
