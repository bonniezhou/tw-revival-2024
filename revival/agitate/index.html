Type Revivals

Agitate

Kurt F. Shaffert

Agitate is a typeface revival inspired by 19th Century newspapers published by American Abolitionist, Frederick Douglass. Based in the sturdy designs of Scotch Roman typefaces, Agitate delivers text with legibility at small sizes and a tried-and-true authority.

Kurt F. Shaffert (aka Cartooning Chaplain) is an American cartoonist, type designer and co-founder of Pearl Press, an independent type foundry and design studio. TypeWest is proving to be a deep dive into theory, praxis and collegiality.
