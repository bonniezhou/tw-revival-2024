My source is from 1768 Germany. It is a manuscript printed by Johann Jakob Adler with Hermann Jacob Lasius as the author. It is metal punches on parchment/vellum. The subject matter is about the Laws of Christianity.

Based in St. Paul, Minnesota, Rishi Murugesan is a designer with a background in Astrophysics. He approaches typeface design with a sense of pragmatism and a sense of wonder.
