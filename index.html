Type Revivals

presented to you by Type West class of 2023. Our class of 20 students studying from various parts of the world and timezones worked tirelessly to revive a historical metal typeface during our first term. Even though they are still a work in progress, we are excited to share our collection of type revivals. We were instructed by Graham Bradley, Fer Cozzi, and Chris Skillern.
