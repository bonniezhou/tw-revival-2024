Type Revivals by TW2024

Type Revivals presented to you by Type West Online class of 2024. Our class of 20 students studying from various parts of the world and timezones worked tirelessly to revive a historical metal typeface during our first term. Even though they are still a work in progress, we are excited to share our collection of type revivals. We were instructed by Sahar Afshar, Michele Petane, Briar Levit, and Ewan Clayton.
