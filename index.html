Type Revivals
Type West 2024 Online Cohort

Type West Class of 2024 presents Type Revival; a collection of revival typefaces based on historical metal type. The typefaces were made during term one of the Type West post-graduate program by students from all over the world. The type designs were done by collecting, researching and analyzing printed materials, sketches and archive collections.

We would like to thank our instructors Sahar Afshar, Michele Patanè, and Ewan Clayton, and our TAs Nora Warschewski, Allie Schmitz, and Kurt F. Shaffert. A special thanks to Gen Ramírez and Rob Saunders for the inspiring workshops.
